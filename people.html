Adam Tsakalidis

Former Organizer
Previously: Queen Mary University of London & The Alan Turing Institute
Adam was a Research Fellow who worked on temporally sensitive methods for natural language processing. His work focused on capturing changes in longitudinal user-generated textual data and studying their association with well-being over time. His research interests spanned semantic change detection, sentiment analysis, and applications in political and mental health domains.
Past Research Interests:
Temporal NLP Semantic Change Detection Sentiment Analysis Mental Health Political Analysis
